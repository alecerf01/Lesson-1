How to make a sandwich

  1. Grab sliced bread and butter both slices
  2. Grab desired filling and place on one slice (ham and cheese)
  3. Add any optional extras
  4. Place other slice on top of selected filling
  5. Cut in half diagonally
  6. Serve on a plate, Voila!
